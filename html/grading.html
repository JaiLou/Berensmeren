Grading

The assesment of the course will be based upon the results of the theoretical exams (T1, T2 en T3) and three practical assignments(P1, P2, P3). The theoretical exams will be made individually and the practical assignments will be made in pairs. Both participants of the pair are responsible for the complete exercise (so the exercise is meant to be made together, not by dividing the work).

The theoretical exams count for 20%, 30% and 50% respectively for the theoretical grade T. The three practical assignments count for 20%, 40% and 40% respectively of the practical grade P.

The final grade is determined by taking the average of T and P, provided that the averge of both are atleast a five. Should either T or P be lower than a five than, than the lowest grade will be become the average grade. The final grade will be rounded to tenth, except when the final grade is between the five and six, if that is the case then the grade will be rounded to whole numbers.

Should the final grade be lower than a 6, but higher than a 4, then you are allowed to make the resit for the theoretical exams or an additional practical assignment. The resit will cover the everything that has been covered in the course and the grade for the resit will replace the lowest grade from T1 t/m T3. The grade for the additional practical assignment will replace the lowest grade from P1 t/m P3.

If due to illness it was not possible to attend an exam or finish a practical assignment, participating in the resit and/or additional practical assignment will be allowed
