Literature

The Science of Equestrian Sports

By Inga Wolframm

This is the main book that we will use during this course. We will discuss every chapter that tackles horse psychology while leaving the ones about the business side of Equastrian sports for you to read in your own time. This book can be bought directly through the site of the publisher or from most online book retailers.

Equine Behaviour: Principles & Practice

By Daniel Mills and Kathryn Nankervis

Equine Behaviour: Principles & Practice will give us an insight in the correct behavior around horses and the optimal ways to take care of and train your horse with regards to a horse's personality. This is the main subject of Part 2 of the book, but we will also discuss small parts of Part 1 and Part 3.

I also recommend buying this book through a populair online retailer since they will almost always offer it for the lowest price available.
